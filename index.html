Day-one demo prompts

These are the prompts I’d put directly in front of Julian, because they map to his Solutions Engineer + Enablement surface area.

“Prep me for a hospital IT review.”

Generate architecture, security, integration, and adoption talking points with citations and gaps to confirm.

“Rewrite this for an Ops VP.”

Translate a technical product explanation into operations outcomes: visibility, automation, waste reduction, and control.

“Answer this questionnaire safely.”

Draft a response, show supporting sources, and flag anything that requires legal, compliance, or product review.

Email is weak here. The move is LinkedIn-first: “I made you a one-page AI enablement map for Movemedical — worth pressure-testing?”
